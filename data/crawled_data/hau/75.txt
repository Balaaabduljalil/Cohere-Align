PDP ta yi rugurugu da APC a kujerun ƴan majalisar  Wakilai, Bilbis ya Kada Marafa a kujerar sanata
Tsohon Ministan yaɗa labaran Ikra Aliyu Bilbis na jam’iyyar PDP ya kada Kabiru Marafa na jam’iyyar APC a zaɓen  kujerar sanatan zamfara ta tsakiya da aka kammala ranar Asabar a Zamfara.
Baturen Zaɓe Farfesa Kabiru Abdullahi ya bayyana cewa Ikra Bilbis ya yi nasara a zaɓen da ƙuri’a 102,866, inda ya doke babban abokin hamayyarsa Sanata Kabiru Marafa na jam’iyyar APC, wanda ya samu Ƙuri’u 91,216.
Haka kuma jam’iyyar PDP ta yi nasarar lashe zaɓen ‘yan majalisar wakilai ta tarayya na mazaɓun Gusau/Tsafe da kuma Gummi/Bukkuyum duk a jihar Zamfara 
A mazaɓar Gusau/Tsafe Kabiru Amadu Maipalace na PDP ya kayar da Sanusi Garba Rikiji na jam’iyyar APC,  wanda shi ne tsohon kakakin majalisar dokokin jihar zamfara.
A mazaɓar Gummi/Bukkuyum, Suleiman Abubakar Gummi na PDP na kayar da Ahmed Mohammed na jami’iyyar APC.
