Kotu ta bada belin mutumin da aka kama da tulin kwayoyin tramadol akan naira miliyan 50
A ranar Juma’a ne babban kotu a jihar Legas ta bada belin Osakwe Evbuomwan kan naira miliyan 50 bayan an kama shi da kilogram 1.55 na kwayoyin tramadol.
Hukumar NDLEA  ce ta shigar da kara bayan an kama Evbuomwan a filin jiragen saman Murtala Mohammed ranar 18 ga Disambar 2021.
Lauyan da ya shigar da karar Augustine Nwagu ya ce an kama Evbuomwan a lokacin da zai shiga jirgin ‘Ethiopian Airline’ ta kofar ‘C’ dauke da kwayoyin.
Nwagu ya ki amincewa da neman belin da lauyan Evbuomwan, Benson Ndakara ya yi saboda har yanzu hukumar na ci gaba da gudanar da bincike akai.
Allagoa ya ce Evbuomwan zai gabatar da shaidu biyu dake da kadarorin a wurin da kotun take.
Sannan ya tabbatar cewa shaidun ma’aikatan gwamnati ne dake mataki 15.
Za a ci gaba da shari’a ranar 18 ga watan Fabrairu.
